Our History

Established in 2005, ECC TECH has been a trusted name in the tech industry for over a decade. Founded by a group of passionate tech enthusiasts, the company started as a small repair shop in the heart of downtown, catering to local residents' needs for reliable and affordable tech solutions.

From Humble Beginnings

The journey began with a modest storefront, where our dedicated team of technicians worked tirelessly to repair computers and phones with precision and care. As word spread about our exceptional service and expertise, our customer base grew, and so did our ambition.

Expansion and Innovation

In 2010, we expanded our services to include the sale of the latest smartphones, laptops, and accessories. This move was driven by our commitment to providing our customers with not only repair solutions but also access to cutting-edge technology.

Embracing the Digital Age

As the digital landscape evolved, so did ECC TECH. In 2015, we launched our first e-commerce website, allowing customers to browse and purchase products from the comfort of their homes. This marked a significant milestone for us, as we ventured into the online marketplace to better serve our tech-savvy clientele.

A Commitment to Quality

Throughout our journey, one thing has remained constant: our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We take pride in offering top-notch products, reliable repairs, and personalized service to every individual who walks through our doors or visits our website.

Community Engagement

ECC TECH isn't just a tech store; we're a part of the community. Over the years, we've sponsored local tech events, hosted workshops on digital literacy, and supported schools with tech donations. Giving back to the community that has supported us is a core value ingrained in our company culture.

Looking Ahead

Today, ECC TECH stands as a symbol of innovation, reliability, and customer-centricity in the tech industry. As we look to the future, we remain committed to staying at the forefront of technological advancements, providing unparalleled service, and being a trusted partner for all your tech needs.

Join us on our journey as we continue to redefine what it means to be a tech store in the digital age.
